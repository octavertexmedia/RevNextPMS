FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Getting Started

How do I create my first property?

Go to Core Management → Properties in the admin panel, click "Add Property", and fill in your hotel details.

What information do I need to get started?

You'll need your hotel name, address, contact information, room types, and base rates to get started.

How long does setup take?

Initial setup typically takes 15-30 minutes. OTA integration may take additional time depending on platform requirements.

Integrations

How do I connect Booking.com?

You'll need your Booking.com extranet credentials. Go to Platform Integrations → Add Property Integration and enter your API credentials.

Which platforms are supported?

We support 75+ platforms including Booking.com, Expedia, Agoda, MakeMyTrip, Goibibo, and many more.

How often does data sync?

By default, data syncs every 5 minutes. You can adjust this in your integration settings.

Billing & Pricing

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, UPI, and bank transfers for Indian customers.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees. You only pay the monthly subscription fee after your 14-day free trial.

Technical Support

How do I access the API?

Generate an API token from your account settings. See our API Reference for documentation.

Do you provide webhooks?

Yes, we support webhooks for real-time notifications. Configure them in your integration settings.

What is your API rate limit?

Rate limits vary by plan. Starter: 1000 req/min, Professional: 5000 req/min, Enterprise: Custom limits.
